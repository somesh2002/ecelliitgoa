ABOUT US
What is E-Cell IIT Goa ?
The Entrepreneurship Cell of IIT Goa is a non-profit organization that aims to inculcate an entrepreneurial mindset in the students. We hope to add fuel to the development of the nation through our various initiatives.
What do we do
We conduct competitions, fests, workshops, and talks in various fields like technology, management, finance, and entrepreneurship, which aim to provide students with a skill set that helps them succeed in their entrepreneurial endeavors.
Support From IIC
A lot of our work is closely linked and collaborated with IIT Goa’s Institute Innovation Council. We would like to extend our gratitude to all its external as well as internal members who not only help us but also motivate and encourage us to conduct student-driven events that help us in our aim for a progressing environment
